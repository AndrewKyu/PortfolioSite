About Me

Here's my story...


Hey, I'm Andrew Kyu and welcome to my website! I was born and raised in the heart of Silicon Valley where I developed a strong interest in technology. As a current student at San Jose State University, pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Engineering, I've established extensive knowledge in both software and hardware development. My passion lies in building innovative DIY gadgets and problem solving, which brings together my creative and technical expertise.

You'll see later on, most of my projects are primarily within the scope of iOS development and web development as both of them are what i'm passionate about. My goal as a developer is to create applications that will better the lives of others around me, and what better way is there to approach this than to use my technical skills to build a better tomorrow.
